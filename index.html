Auto Layout needs to be properly understood and applied systematically, you have to understand what every single constraint is doing, throwing them in at random until its all blue is never going to work.


Making Sense of Auto Layout focuses on the fundamentals of Auto Layout including how it decides the position and size of a UI Element (UIView, UILabel etc), what problems do Auto Layout solve, why do Xcode shows you red lines and how to troubleshoot them.
